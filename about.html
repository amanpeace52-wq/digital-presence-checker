My story

Why I care about your digital life

I did not step into cyber security because I love machines. I stepped in because I kept watching ordinary people — parents, students, small business owners — quietly fall behind a digital world that moves faster than their confidence.

In my work as a lecturer and PhD researcher, the same sentence keeps coming up: “I feel exposed, but I do not even know what I should be worried about.”

I saw people trying their best — using phones, apps, AI tools and social media — but still feeling like there was a secret rule book they never got to read. That feeling of “everyone else seems to know what they are doing” can be very lonely.

Turning complexity into calm

My work sits in the middle of a few worlds: academic research, teaching, cyber security, user experience and AI. I translate what is happening in those worlds into:

  • Simple language that makes sense in everyday life.
  • Tools that help you see your own patterns and risks.
  • Services and coaching that give you a human partner, not just another app.

What this space is for

This site is a small, calm corner of the internet where you can:

  • Look at your digital footprint without shame or panic.
  • Understand how AI and online systems affect you and your family.
  • Get practical, human help if you want someone to walk through it with you.

If you would like to talk, you can always start with a short message on the contact page. You do not have to have the right words — you just have to say, “This is what is on my mind.”
