Gentle 1:1 digital presence coaching

A calm space to talk about your digital life, website, AI use or online safety — with someone who speaks both human and tech.

Online sessions · Flexible formats
What we can explore together
  • Mapping your digital footprint and cleaning up old accounts.
  • Reviewing your website for clarity, safety and first impressions.
  • Talking through AI tools you already use (or are afraid to try).
  • Setting gentle boundaries around screen time and digital overwhelm.

Session formats

  • Starter session (45 minutes): One focused topic, quick wins.
  • Deep-dive (90 minutes): Full review + step-by-step plan.
  • Ongoing support: A series of sessions over a few weeks.

How to book

  1. Send a short message through the contact page with your main concern.
  2. We agree on a format and time that fits your life and timezone.
  3. You receive a confirmation with call details and a simple pre-session prompt.

You do not need the “perfect” description of your problem. A few honest lines about what feels confusing or heavy is enough.
