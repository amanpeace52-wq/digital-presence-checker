Coaching

Cyber literacy coaching — one to one

Private, practical sessions for students, parents, professionals and small business owners. Especially helpful if you feel behind with AI, apps and modern online life.

Session options

  • 30 minutes — $30: basics, scams, passwords, phone safety.
  • 45 minutes — $45: identity, privacy, social media and AI risks.

How coaching can help you

  • Turn vague anxiety into a clear, simple plan.

  • Understand how AI and new tools affect your life, not just in theory.
  • Leave with two or three concrete actions you can take immediately.
