My Story (Why I Do This)

I didn’t start in cyber security because I loved machines. I started because I kept seeing people — smart, hardworking people — feeling powerless and confused online.

As a student, as a teacher, and later as a PhD researcher, I watched how easily people’s digital lives could get messy, stressful, and sometimes unsafe — without them even realising it.

Parents worried about their children. Business owners unsure who could see their data. Students overwhelmed by passwords, scams and privacy.

So I decided to focus on something simple: making digital safety and clarity human again. Not scary. Not overly technical. Just real, calm, and practical.

This space is where I do that — one person, one website, one digital life at a time.
