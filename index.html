My Story

I didn’t begin in cyber security because I loved technology. I began because I watched normal people — parents, students, small business owners — get quietly overwhelmed by a digital world that keeps changing faster than they can keep up.

Through teaching, research and real conversations, I saw the same worry again and again: “I feel exposed, but I don’t even know where to start.”

That question became my work. This space is my way of turning complex cyber concepts into calm, human language so you can make better choices without needing to become “techy”.

AI Is Powerful — And It’s Changing Digital Risk

Some simple numbers to sit with:

  • ⚠️ A large majority of breaches now involve human error — not just “hackers”.
  • ⚠️ AI tools make it easier to create fake emails, fake voices and deepfake videos.
  • ⚠️ The average person has data spread across dozens of apps and platforms.
  • ⚠️ Many people reuse the same password or slight variations across everything.

AI itself is not “good” or “bad”. But it does amplify whatever is already weak in our digital habits — and that’s why simple digital hygiene, cyber literacy and clear online identity matter more than ever.
