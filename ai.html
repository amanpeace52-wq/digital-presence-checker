What this space covers

AI in your everyday life

AI already touches your search results, social feeds, photos, translation, maps and more. Sometimes it helps, sometimes it adds noise, and sometimes it quietly shifts power in ways that are not obvious.

My work in AI literacy is not about turning everyone into a “power user”. It is about helping you see where AI is involved, how it might shape your choices, and what you can do to stay in charge.

Topics we explore

  • What AI actually is (and what it is not).
  • Where AI shows up in your daily apps and tools.
  • How AI might use your data and digital footprint.
  • How to use AI in safer, more intentional ways.

Ways to learn with me

  • 1:1 sessions: Ask anything in a private, judgment-free space.
  • Family / parent sessions: Talk about children, teens and AI use.
  • Workplace / student workshops: Gentle introductions to AI tools and risks.

If you’d like a session or workshop tailored to you, you can start from the coaching page or send a message on the contact page.
